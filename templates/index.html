Frequently Asked Questions

What makes Pet Haven different from other pet services? +
At Pet Haven, we combine luxury care with genuine love for animals. Our staff undergoes rigorous training and our facilities are designed with pet comfort in mind. We offer personalized care plans, 24/7 monitoring, and premium amenities that set us apart from standard pet care providers.
What safety measures do you have in place? +
Safety is our top priority. We have secure facilities with monitored access, 24/7 surveillance, trained staff certified in pet first aid, strict vaccination requirements, and emergency protocols with nearby veterinary partnerships. All playgroups are carefully supervised and matched by temperament.
How do I prepare my pet for boarding? +
Bring your pet's food to maintain their regular diet, any medications with instructions, a favorite toy or blanket for comfort, and proof of vaccinations. We recommend a trial day stay for first-timers. Complete our pet profile form to help us understand your pet's routine and preferences.
What are your vaccination requirements? +
Dogs require Rabies, DHPP, and Bordetella. Cats need Rabies and FVRCP. All vaccines must be current (within 1 year for annual vaccines). We accept titer test results in some cases. Please provide records from your veterinarian before your first visit.
How to register if you are a service provider? +
We're always looking for passionate pet professionals! Visit our careers page to apply online. Requirements include relevant experience, certifications (for groomers/trainers), clean background check, and completion of our training program. We offer competitive compensation and a supportive work environment.
What is your data privacy policy? +
We take data security seriously. Client information is stored securely and never shared with third parties without consent. We use encryption for online transactions and limit staff access to sensitive data. You may request account deletion anytime. Full details are available in our Privacy Policy document.
