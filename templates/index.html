FAQs

What is Pet Haven? +
Pet Haven is a premier pet care service provider dedicated to offering the best care for your furry friends. We provide a range of services including grooming, boarding, walking, and veterinary care.
What services do we provide? +
We offer a comprehensive range of pet care services including grooming, boarding, pet sitting, dog walking, training, and veterinary services. We also provide specialized care for pets with special needs.
How to register if you are a service provider? +
If you're interested in joining our team of service providers, please visit our careers page or contact us directly. We require relevant experience, certifications (where applicable), and a passion for animal care.
